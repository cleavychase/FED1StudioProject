Meet our staff

Sunny Side strives to bring the best teaching staff around to brighten your child's life. Our teachers are the best around!

Executive Director: Lee Unkrich

Lee Ulrich has been our director since the dawn of Sunny Side! He gives our school it's upbeat feel and keeps us focused on our vision of happy children, families, and staff.

Assistant Executive Director: John Lasseter

John supports our operations and was a founding member of our team. He helps ensure we stay on the path to early childhood success.

Principal Andrew Stanton

Principal Stanton supports our teaching staff and regularly audits classroom activity to find new and exciting ways to enrich the education of our students.
