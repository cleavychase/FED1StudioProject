About Us

We are the area's leading early education day school. We've had the oportunity to enrich the lives of hundreds of local children and their families and are eager to bring independent education to your child. We are losely styled on the Montesori method and focus heavily on independent learning through play. Whether its builing up interpersonal skills, creative expression, or early mathematics, we strive to grow independent minds in an upbeat and engaging environment.
