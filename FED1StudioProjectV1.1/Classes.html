We divide our teachings into three age groupings or classes:

  • Children from ages 2 to 3 learn in the Caterpillar Room
  • ages 3 to 4 are in our Chrysalis Room
  • ages 4 to kindergarten children learn in our open concept Butterfly Room.

Our curriculum falls into the following 6 categories:

  • Mathematical learning
  • Language learning
  • Interpersonal learning
  • Fine Motor Learning
  • Sensorial Learning
  • Music and Art

Due to social distancing requirements, we have adjusted out normal scheduling. Caterpillars (ages 2-3) meet on Mondays. Chrysalis kids (ages 3-4) meet on Wednesdays and Thursdays. Butterflies (ages 4 to Kindergarten) meet on Fridays and Saturdays. Each course curriculum section is set in a two hour block. We rotate scheduling to ensure our morning students and afternoon students recieve a full curriculum lesson each two week period.
